title: "Compose a Message"
icon: "message"
---
A message is a response your Agent will communicate to your callers based on defined time logic.

For example, if your business closes for lunch everyday from 12-1pm, a custom "out to lunch" message would instruct the Agent to respond with your desired out to lunch message and behavior.

Click [here](https://docs.syllable.ai/Resources/Messages) to learn even more about messages
