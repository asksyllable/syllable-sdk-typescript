title: "Create a custom message"
icon: "message"
---
A custom message is a message or response your Agent will say to your callers based on time logic. 

For example, if your business closes for lunch everyday from 12-1pm, a custom message would instruct the Agent to respond with your desired out to lunch message and behavior. 

Click [here](https://docs.syllable.ai/Resources/CustomMessages) to learn even more about custom messages
